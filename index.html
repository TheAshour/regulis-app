How It Works

Three steps from regulatory standards to full audit readiness

1

Import Your Standards

Load your regulatory framework — IOSA, ICAO, FAA, EASA, or a custom standard. All requirements are structured, cross-referenced, and ready for analysis.

2

Upload & Map Evidence

Upload your operational manuals as PDFs up to 50MB. AI ingests, indexes, and maps each section to your requirements — surfacing gaps and coverage status instantly.

3

Close Gaps & Publish

Prioritised recommendations drive remediation. Track progress, assign actions, simulate your audit, and publish your compliance package — ready for any regulator.
